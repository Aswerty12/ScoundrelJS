Quick Rules Reminder

Scoundrel in one screen.

  • Each room has up to 4 cards. Resolve 3 cards per full room.
  • You may run away once per turn; the room goes to the deck bottom in chosen order.
  • Hearts are potions: only the first potion each turn heals; extras are discarded.
  • Diamonds are weapons: equipping a new one discards the old weapon and its slain stack.
  • Monsters (clubs/spades): fight barehanded for full value damage, or with weapon for max(monster - weapon, 0).
  • With a weapon, you can only use it on monsters with value less than or equal to your last weapon kill.
  • Final partial room: run away is disabled; resolve all remaining cards.
