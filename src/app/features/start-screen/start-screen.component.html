Scoundrel Card Game

Survive the dungeon with 20 health. Resolve cards from each room, manage weapon restrictions, and avoid getting cornered in the final partial room.

Rules Snapshot

  • Clubs/Spades are monsters. Diamonds are weapons. Hearts are potions.
  • Red face cards and red aces are removed from the deck.
  • Resolve exactly 3 cards from a full room of 4; remaining card carries over.
  • Only one potion heals each turn; extra potion cards are discarded.
  • Running away is allowed, but never twice in a row and never in final partial rooms.

Ready to Enter the Dungeon?

Cards use original PNG assets from the local playing-cards folder and follow the official Scoundrel ruleset.
